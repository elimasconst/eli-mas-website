About Us

ELI MAS Construction, Inc. was founded by Eliseo Camas Agualema, who started his journey in the trades as a young man. Originally established in 2005 as J.B.C. Construction, the company has grown into a trusted name in New York City construction.

Built on a foundation of hard work, skill, and reliability, our mission is to deliver high-quality home improvement services with honesty and care.

We specialize in concrete sidewalks and driveways, masonry, paver installations, and in-home renovations tailored to our clients' needs.

Proudly serving residential, commercial, and public clients across all five boroughs of New York City.
