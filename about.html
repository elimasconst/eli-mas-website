About Us

ELI MAS Construction, Inc. was founded by Eliseo Camas Agualema, who began his career in the trades at a young age. Originally launched in the mid-2000s as J.B.C. Construction, the company has grown into a trusted name in New York City construction.

Built on a foundation of hard work, skill, and reliability, our mission is to provide high-quality home improvement services with honesty and care.

We specialize in concrete sidewalks and driveways, masonry, paver installations, and in-home renovations — all tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

We proudly serve residential, commercial, and public clients across all five boroughs of New York City.
